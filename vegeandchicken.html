Vegetable Salad and Chicken

Prep time: 20 mins

Yield: 9

Price: R82.50

Steps

  1. When coating and breading your chicken, be sure to tap off the excess buttermilk as well as the excess flour.
  2. Give your chicken a gentle pat after coating with the flour mixture to make sure that the flour sticks to the buttermilk.
  3. Make sure that you don’t overcrowd your pan. You want to make sure that the chicken isn’t bumping against other pieces of chicken which can knock off the breading.
  4. Only flip your chicken once while frying. The more you fuss with your chicken, the more likely you are to knock off or disturb some of the crunchy coating
