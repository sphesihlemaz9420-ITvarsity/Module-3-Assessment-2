Steps

  1. In a blender, puree enough watermelon chunks so that you have 4 cups of watermelon puree. Add lime juice and agave and mix again. Pour into 4 cups. Top with sparkling water.
  2. Add lime juice and agave and mix again. Pour into 4 cups. Top with sparkling water.
  3. Combine and shake all ingredients except ginger beer in a shaker tin with ice, and then strain over fresh ice.
