Fried Mashroom

Prep time: 20 mins

Yield: 9

Price: R82.50

Extra Requests




Steps

  1. Wash the mushrooms and remove most of the stem.
  2. Pat mushrooms dry with a paper towel.
  3. Coat mushrooms with flour, lightly tap to remove excess flour.
  4. Heat the oil in the deep fryer to 375 degrees. Pay close attention to grease while it heats.
  5. Deep fry mushrooms in batches for approximately 3- 4 minutes or until golden brown.
